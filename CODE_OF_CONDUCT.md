# Code of Conduct

AccessGate follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and security-minded.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent, but be precise about risks.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Treat security reports and operational details carefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing secrets, credentials, private infrastructure details, or exploit instructions outside the responsible disclosure process.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the project maintainers privately. For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
